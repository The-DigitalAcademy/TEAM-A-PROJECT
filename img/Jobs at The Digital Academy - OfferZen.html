Summary

What we do

The Digital Academy recruits and trains young up and coming software developers for our corporate partners. This is done in a work simulated, project-based environment where developers build projects that matter over a 4-6 month time horizon.

Why Work For Us

We're a rapidly growing, socially focused startup which is having a big impact in the digital skills economy through upskilling young development talent and placing them into jobs.

Our Culture

We're a very deliverable focused organisation and all management and oversight is based on meeting reasonable deliverables.

Our Engineering Processes

We are project focused and work in an agile environment. We're looking at bringing in someone to help craft and shape our engineering and project management processes.

Our Hiring Process

Demonstrate skills, meet the founders, get hired.
